About the System

The Digital Health Insurance Claims Management and Tracking System streamlines healthcare insurance operations by integrating patients, healthcare providers and administrators into a secure digital ecosystem.

Purpose

Digitize health insurance claim processing while reducing paperwork, delays, fraud and human error.

Vision

Create an efficient healthcare claims ecosystem where every stakeholder can securely access accurate information in real time.

Mission

Improve transparency, accountability and efficiency through a centralized digital health insurance platform.

System Features

Everything required to manage healthcare insurance claims digitally.

Patient Portal

Register, submit claims, upload documents, track claim progress and file inquiries.

Healthcare Provider

Create claims, verify patient information and manage supporting documentation.

Insurance Provider

Approve claims, verify documents, handle inquiries and generate reports.

Administrator

Manage patient, provider and insurance staff accounts and oversee system access.

Claims Processing

Efficient electronic healthcare claim submission and verification.

Document Upload

Upload prescriptions, receipts, medical reports and invoices.

Inquiries

Patients can raise inquiries while insurance providers respond digitally.

Notifications

Automatic notifications keep every stakeholder updated throughout the claim lifecycle.

Reports & Analytics

Generate comprehensive healthcare claim reports for informed decision making.
